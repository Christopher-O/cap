Making a payment to the CRA

Interest and penalties

If you do not pay an amount that is due on time, the CRA may apply interest from the day your payment was due.

You can find our quarterly interest rates to use when calculating interest on a balance you owe.

If you are scheduling a series of pre-authorized payments to pay a debt you owe, you can calculate the estimated interest in My Account or My Business Account.

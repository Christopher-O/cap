Payments to the CRA

Call if you are unable to pay in full or on time

We understand that people and businesses sometimes find themselves in circumstances where they are unable to pay in full and on time.

By giving us a call, you can let us know you need more time and we can help you:

  • Defer a repayment to a later date
  • Start or change a payment arrangement
  • Find out how to minimize interest and penalties
  • Discuss other ways to reduce or resolve your debt

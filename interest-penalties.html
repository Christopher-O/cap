Calculating interest on a balance owing

If you do not pay an amount that is due on time, the CRA may apply interest from the day your payment was due.

[Interest is not applied to debts you may owe because of overpayments of Canada child benefit or personal GST/HST credit.]

[CRA's interest rates change each quarter.] Look up our quarterly interest rates to calculate interest on a debt.

If you are scheduling a series of pre-authorized payments to pay a debt, you can calculate the estimated interest in My Account or My Business Account.

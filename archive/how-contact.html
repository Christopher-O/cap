Making a payment to the CRA

How we will contact you about a debt

[Normally, we notify you of a balance you owe on a Notice of Assessment, Notice of Reassessment, or Notice of Determination. If you continue to have a balance owing, we may contact you by phone or in person.]

Make sure it's really the CRA contacting you before giving out your personal information: What to expect when the CRA contacts you

If you owe an individual tax or benefit debt with the CRA for more than 90 days after we notify you, CRA collections will:

  • make 3 attempts to give you a verbal legal warning by phone
  • send 1 written legal warning letter

If you owe a business or corporate debt, in some cases we may begin collections actions right after you are notified of your debt.

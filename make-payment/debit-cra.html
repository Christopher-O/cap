How to pay the amount you owe

My Payment is a secure electronic Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) service that lets you make payments directly to the CRA using your bank access card (Interac Debit, Visa Debit, or Debit Mastercard). 

You cannot use credit cards with My Payment. For credit card payment options, go to Pay by credit or debit card, PayPal, or Interac e-Transfer.

Fees

The CRA does not charge a fee for using this service.

Ask your bank or credit union about any fees they may charge for making online payments, as well as any transaction limits.

Hours of service

My Payment is available 21 hours a day, seven days a week.

Secure access to My Payment

Make sure you have a secure internet connection and your browser is up to date when you access CRA’s My Payment and other online services:

  • Access online services safely
  • Your browser

You will not be able to access CRA’s secure online services if you use an older browser that does not support the latest security standards.
